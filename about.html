About ASKBRO

Learn about the research, goals, and the people behind the project.

Project Overview

ASKBRO is an AI-powered educational assistant created to help students enhance their learning experience. It provides instant answers, explanations, and guidance on academic topics, encouraging independent study and better comprehension of lessons.

Objectives

  • To develop a responsive website for AI-based student support.
  • To help students understand lessons through clear, step-by-step answers.
  • To promote digital literacy and self-learning through technology.
  • To explore how AI can assist students in improving productivity and academic performance.
